Ambit’s revenue cycle management (RCM) consulting stems from 12 years of extensive experience managing every aspect of the revenue cycle, including optimization, for more than 250 healthcare institutions.

We offer a thorough evaluation of your practice’s billing environment and overall financial health.

Our RCM consulting team will examine your billing platform and clearinghouse, collaborate with your team, and analyze all financial data to deliver a detailed assessment. We will identify key factors, pain points, and bad debts affecting your business, and provide tailored optimization solutions.

WE ARE HIGHLY EXPERIENCED IN ANALYZING AND IDENTIFYING REVENUE CYCLE PROBLEMS

Our assessments are tailored to meet the specific needs of the client and may include:

All the below given aspects should be with check boxes

  • Review of in-house compliance programs and CPT coding for billing procedures.
  • Analysis of revenue loss from medical necessities, with recommended changes to follow-up procedures to ensure greater compliance.
  • Detailed analysis of billing and collection history to identify areas for improvement.
  • Examination of fee schedules, provider arrangements, and CPT coding, with recommendations to address issues where claims are denied or inadequately paid.
  • Operational reviews of billing functions to assess the appropriateness of systems and resources.
  • Review of internal processes with recommendations for streamlining patient registration, correcting missing information, and enhancing bill collection efforts.

The cost of our operational assessment is determined by the time needed to complete the agreed-upon scope of work. If your practice opts to contract with Ambit for additional billing services, the assessment fee may be reduced or prorated.

We are confident that our comprehensive report and follow-up recommendations will lead to significant cost savings and opportunities for revenue recovery for your practice.
