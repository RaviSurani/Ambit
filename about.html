About Us

Founded in 2012, Ambit Global Solution was conceived by a visionary entrepreneur with a mission

TO BE BETTER TO DO BETTER & TO LEAVE BETTER

TO BE BETTER

When asked for a fitting tagline for Ambit Global Solution, founder Nishant Sharma responded almost instantly:

"It's the pursuit..." A pursuit of what can be...

Founded in 2012, Ambit Global Solution is among the earliest divisions of the Ambit Group, which also includes Ambit taxation & Accounting as well. The core idea is straightforward: Empower and Enable.

TO Do BETTER

Nishant founded Ambit Global Solution in 2012 and has since served as its Managing Director.

Beginning with just one client in 2012—whom we still retain today—Ambit Global Solution has grown to serve a diverse range of top-tier healthcare institutions, hospitals, physician groups, laboratories, DME companies, and independent physicians, dental clinics & hospitals. We are honored to have played a role in advancing and improving the businesses of numerous healthcare professionals and organizations.

The vision was to develop a comprehensive RCM services module by consolidating all RCM sub-segments under one roof. This includes everything from patient registration and billing & coding functions to appeals, denial management, payment reconciliations, patient statements, and credit card processing.

At Ambit, we ensure that once you become a client, you receive a personalized Practice Management Concierge service rather than just a standard RCM company.

With 12+ years of extensive multi-specialty experience, we recognized the need to focus on areas where we excel. In 2018, we introduced Ambit's specialized Laboratory RCM module, tailored specifically for mid-sized and large clinical and molecular laboratories.

Leadership

Nishant Sharma

Nishant Sharma (Founder and Managing Director AMBIT )

Nishant founded Ambit Global Solutions in 2012 and has served as its Managing Director since then. Starting with a single small client in 2012—whom we still serve today—Ambit grew to over 300 clients nationwide by 2019. In 2018, Nishant expanded the company’s expertise by adding a Laboratory Billing division, which included the establishment of six specialized Lab RCM departments, significantly advancing Ambit's reputation as a leading provider of Laboratory Billing services. His hands-on approach to every aspect of the business, regardless of size, and her personal connection with each client, are complemented by his warm and altruistic nature.

Outside of business, Nishant enjoys playing Cricket, particularly admiring the hard hitter (you can guess who we're referring to). They’re also passionate about coffee, delighting in their workday with excellent espresso.
