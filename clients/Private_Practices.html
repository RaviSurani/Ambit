Private Practices Medical Billing Services We Offer

Documentation errors are the primary issue affecting Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) in private practices. At Ambit Global Solution, we ensure your team is well-trained and understands the critical importance of accurate documentation. Proper documentation significantly improves the likelihood of claim acceptance, and with our 99.9% acceptance rate on billed claims, you can trust us to handle any denials promptly by correcting and resubmitting them. We file all claims within 24 hours.

In addition to improving documentation, we assist with gathering information on insurance eligibility and benefits. This helps prevent overcharging due to lack of information, which can erode patient trust and lead to refunds.

We also keep you updated on coding and compliance to prevent revenue loss from incorrect code usage. Ambit Global Solution will provide guidance on the latest codes and best practices to maximize your revenue.

Our services extend to sending patient statements, answering calls, and managing appeals. By taking care of these time-consuming tasks, we free up your staff to focus on patient care, enhancing both patient satisfaction and practice efficiency.
