PRIVACY POLICY FOR AMBIT GLOBAL SOLUTION

1.Introduction

At Ambit Global Solution (AGS), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard the information we obtain from healthcare providers and organizations via our website (https://www.ambitglobalsolution.com) and our services (collectively, "Services"). This policy is designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant privacy laws.

2.Information We Collect

  • Organizational Information


    We gather details about the healthcare organizations we assist, including contact information, service needs, and other relevant business data.

  • Healthcare Data


    As a service provider to healthcare entities, we may handle patient data on behalf of our clients. This includes sensitive health information as defined by the GDPR.

  • Technical and Usage Data


    We automatically collect information about your use of our Services, such as IP addresses, browser types, and activity logs.

  • Contact Information


    By providing your contact details, including email addresses and phone numbers, you consent to receive communications from us via email, phone, and SMS. This information is used solely to provide the services you request and will not be shared with third parties.

3.Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent


    We may process data with your explicit consent for specific purposes.

  • Contractual Necessity


    Processing is essential for fulfilling our service contracts with healthcare organizations.

  • Legal Obligation


    Processing is required to meet legal obligations.

  • Legitimate Interests


    We process data to pursue legitimate interests, such as improving our services and ensuring their security, provided these interests do not override your rights and freedoms.

4.Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and enhance our Services.

  • Fulfill our contractual obligations.

  • Communicate effectively with clients.

  • Ensure the security and integrity of our data.

  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

5.Data Subject Rights

Under the GDPR, you have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, and transfer your data. You also have the right to object to certain processing activities. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly.

6.Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized or unlawful processing, as well as from accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

7.Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, including fulfilling legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

8.International Transfers

Personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When transferring data outside the EEA, we ensure that it is protected by implementing suitable safeguards.

9.Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of these changes by updating the policy date and, where applicable, providing more prominent notices.

10.Contact Information

For questions about these Terms, please contact us at: legal@ambitglobalsolution.com.
