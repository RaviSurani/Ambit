Your specialty is our specialty. As insurance companies have expanded the range of services they cover, you can now offer your patients better care at more affordable rates. However, this increased variety can make it more challenging to secure acceptance into these networks. Ambit Global Solution is here to help you navigate this process and obtain contracts that work in your favor.

Thanks to our established relationships with numerous insurance companies and networks, we can negotiate advantageous contracts on your behalf, including better rates for the services you provide. Understanding the complexities of contracting and credentialing is crucial, as securing the best rates from your desired network is a full-time job.

Having the right contract is key to building a successful relationship with your preferred networks. Starting with a contract that leads to revenue loss can negatively impact your business and be difficult to amend once established. Ambit Medical Billing leverages our connections to secure the best possible terms for our clients.
