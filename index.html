Frequently Asked Questions

We know you might have questions, and we're here to help! In this section, we've gathered answers to the most common inquiries our clients have. Whether you're wondering about our services, processes, pricing, or how we can assist with your specific needs, you'll find helpful information here.

What services does Ambit Global Solution provide?

Ambit Global Solution offers comprehensive medical billing services, including claims submission, payment posting, revenue cycle management, denial management, and patient billing. We also provide consulting services to optimize your practice's financial performance.

How does Ambit Global Solution ensure data security and HIPAA compliance?

We prioritize the security of our clients' data by using encrypted communications, secure servers, and regular security audits. All our processes are HIPAA-compliant to ensure patient privacy and data protection.

What software does Ambit Global Solution use for medical billing?

We use industry-leading medical billing software that is compliant with all relevant regulations, including HIPAA. Our systems are regularly updated to incorporate the latest coding and billing standards.

How does Ambit Global Solution charge for its services?

We offer flexible pricing models based on the needs of your practice. Our fees are typically a percentage of collections, but we also offer flat-rate and hybrid pricing options. We provide a transparent cost breakdown during our initial consultation.

Is there a contract required to work with Ambit Global Solution?

Yes, we typically require a service agreement that outlines the scope of work, pricing, and terms of service. However, we offer flexible contract terms to suit the needs of our clients.
