Urgent Care Billing Services We Provide

We provide effective urgent care billing services designed to meet the unique billing needs of urgent care facilities, as detailed below:

Accurate Data Entry

We collect and record precise information on urgent care centers in a database. Our data entry team ensures this information is accurately entered into the system to prevent errors during the billing process.

Completion of Claims

With years of expertise in urgent care revenue cycle management, we offer exceptional scalability and flexibility in both electronic and paper claim submissions, adhering to the insurance carrier's requirements.

Reports for Practices

We provide regular reports and updates tailored to the specific needs of urgent care practices or facilities, delivered via email.

Payments and Audits

Upon receipt of payments and explanations from clients, we promptly enter all details into our systems. We also conduct payment audits to ensure accurate and optimal reimbursements for your urgent care facilities.

We keep aging reports current and follow up on claims submitted to insurance companies to ensure timely reimbursement.

Ambit Global Solution Urgent Care Revenue Cycle Management Process

As an independent facility, we recognize your goals and are well-equipped to help you achieve them. Ambit Global Solution has provided innovative medical billing services to hospitals, emergency rooms, and urgent care centers throughout the United States, enhancing cash flow, reducing costs, and significantly improving and streamlining revenue cycle management. Key steps in our urgent care RCM process include:

  • Super bills/health records are acquired from the provider’s office and extensively analyzed and evaluated to ensure proper billing measures are implemented.
  • Verify demographics and investigate insurance benefits
  • Chart abstractions are performed by CPCs (Certified Professional Coders). Medical and diagnostic data are used to assign CPT codes and modifiers.
  • Claims that have coded directed to the charge-entry department.
  • Claim audits are performed to check that completed claims correspond to Medicare and HIPAA rules.
  • Claims are forwarded to the insurances via clearinghouse.
  • Rejected claims are corrected and resubmitted by the denial management department.
  • Follow up on claims within 30 calendar days.
  • We post ERAs and EOBs into the system.
  • Our AR follow up department monitors outstanding medical claims.
