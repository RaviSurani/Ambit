Hospital Medical Billing Services We Provide

We offer comprehensive hospital billing services along with customized solutions that include the following:

Insurance Eligibility Verification Services

We verify that patients are eligible for hospital benefits under their insurance policies and check for any pre-authorization or referral requirements. We also calculate deductibles and co-payments. Our services improve cash flow, enhance reimbursements, and streamline eligibility verification.

Demographics & Charge Entry Services

Charge entry, a critical step in the billing process, determines the payment amount for the healthcare organization. We enter charges from the patient’s FaceSheet into their account. Our team ensures that patient demographics and hospital charges are entered accurately, providing high-quality and error-free services.

Hospital Billing & Analysis

Our billing team reviews various sources in a patient’s file—such as doctor’s notes, diagnostic test reports, and imaging reports—to verify services rendered anh3 apply appropriate codes. Accurate coding is crucial to prevent claim denials, underpayments, and workflow disruptions.

AR Follow-up Services

Managing accounts receivable can be complex and time-consuming. We diligently follow up on unpaid hospital claims to minimize AR days and ensure timely processing of refused and underpaid claims. Our team also appeals denials promptly to facilitate proper reimbursement.

Hospital Collection Services & Denial Analysis

Denial of claims can lead to significant losses. Our collection services team investigates the reasons for denials, corrects errors, and resubmits claims within the filing deadlines. We address information gaps swiftly and analyze denial causes to improve processes and reduce future denials.

Payment Posting

Payment posting is crucial for identifying payer issues such as denials for medical necessity or non-covered services. We review ERAs and scanned EOBs (Explanation of Benefits) to ensure accurate entry of payment details into the system and update patient accounts accordingly.

Providers Credentialing

Credentialing is essential for associating providers with payers, enabling patients to use insurance for medical services and ensuring providers receive payment. Our team streamlines this process, setting up ERA, increasing referrals, reducing revenue leakage, and addressing denial trends.

Out of Network Negotiation

Our strategic approach to out-of-network claims maximizes savings. We use specialized algorithms and comprehensive supplier databases to identify cost-saving opportunities and ensure adequate reimbursement. Our team works diligently on each claim to secure the highest possible reimbursement.
